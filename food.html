Days Without Pounded Yam Aren't Days At All!


Pounded yam and vegetable soup are the yin and yang of food haven, they're like two peas in a pod, they go side by side, one cannot exist without the other

These ninja dojos - the fluffiness of the yam combined with the rich nutrients from the vegetable could paint your entire day with beautiful colours, and you can absolutely choose any vegetable soup of your choice - Egusi, Efo riro, Ila alasepo, Oha soup, Edi kaikong


Don't worry, I'll be sharing easy-peasy tips on how to make it



Materials Needed
S/N NAME QTY
1 Yam One
2 Mortar/Pestle or Machine One
3 Pot One
4 Gas/Stove One
Just a simple DIY


How to Prepare:


  • Cut and peel Yam
  • Wash thoroughly
  • Place in a pot and boil until soft
  • Pound or grind (however preferred)
  • Add water inbetween
  • Pound or grind
  • Serve with any correct vegetable soup.
